Store Policies

Financing Policies:

  1. We offer our customers a 5% discount on credit purchases if paid within 10 days
  2. If paid after 10 days, no discount is given
  3. All outstanding credit purchases must be paid within 30 days

Delivery Policies:

The USPS is Durham House of Pets preferred carrier. Standard carrier charges apply.

Return Policies:

Due to the nature of our goods, returns will not be accepted unless the product is unopened, and receipt of purchase is present. All returns must be completed within 30 days of purchase, no exceptions.
