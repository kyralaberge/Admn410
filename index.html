Who We Are

History:

The Smiths opened the doors to Durham House of Pets in 1899. Located on Main St Durham, New Hampshire, founder Herald Smith saw a need for a pet supply store in the community after his family adopted a sweet elderly golden retriever named Lucy. The shelves were stocked with many different brands of food and toys.

Over the years, some of the products offered have changed but one thing stays the same across all locations, a warm welcome and a caring staff to help you with all your pet supply needs.

Why Choose Us?

With over 30 years of experience in the pet supply industry we know to keep our customers and their pets happy. Conveniently located throughout the state, we make it easy for you to get quality supplies for your family pets.

It is our mission to give all of our stores a community feel, and to prove it, we make an effort to get to know our customers, and their pets of course, so that we can be better tailored to them and their needs. We hope to one day be a part of every pet loving family!
